The Typing Detective

How fast can you type? Let's find out!

This game allows you to practice your typing skills with a quote from Sherlock Holmes.
Click "START" to begin!

Rules:

  1. Type each word as it appears in the highlighted section.
  2. Press the spacebar after each word to submit it.
  3. Pay attention to punctuation—every comma, full-stop, and character counts!
  4. Match the case exactly (uppercase and lowercase).
  5. The timer starts as soon as you hit "START"—type as fast and accurately as you can!
